## Project Summary

### What is this project for?

The Jewelment B2B Platform is a luxury digital showroom, wholesale e-commerce portal, and internal CRM system. It is designed as a scalable, multi-tenant SaaS platform where businesses like Ashok Jewels (one of the clients) can bring their own databases (BYODB) and operate under their own branding. It features a complete Product Information Management (PIM) system, dynamic margin automation tied to live bullion rates, and Server-Driven UI (SDUI) for mobile apps.

### How does this solve the problem?

Traditional B2B jewelry wholesale heavily relies on manual purchase orders, static pricing, and offline printed catalogs. The Jewelment platform completely solves those inefficiencies by:

  1. Frictionless Ordering: Replacing manual POs with a highly visual "PO Matrix Checkout" and "Flipbook" presentation layer.
  2. Profitability Protection: Utilizing a dynamic margin calculator tied to live MCX bullion rates to automatically adjust tiered pricing and discounts.
  3. Sales Empowerment: Giving field sales reps powerful CRM tools, including the ability to "impersonate" clients to place orders on their behalf.
  4. Scalable SaaS Infrastructure: By leveraging PostgreSQL, Edge subdomain routing, and Server-Driven UI (SDUI), it allows the entire ecosystem to be safely segmented and sold to other businesses as a service.

Ready to Ship Solution

Whenever a customer orders any products, our system verifies that according to the customer PIN code in which area and in which store the product is available to ship instantly — this is known as a ready-to-ship product. In case we don't have the product ready to ship then we raise the order for manufacturing.

Extra Gold Solution

Whenever the user orders something then the system will check if the order is ready for shipping or we need to manufacture this. If the order is ready to ship then we need to make sure whatever metal weight we show on a website is the product real weight.

If the product gold weight in master inventory is 3.5g but after production in store it is 3.3g, then 0.2g difference means we re-initiate the customer's payment as a refund.

If the store product is 3.8g (0.3g extra), then we send a mail/WhatsApp to the customer informing them. If they agree to pay the difference, the order continues. If they decline, we initiate custom re-manufacturing.

Disclaimer: We always will add the disclaimer — if there is a difference between the gold weight then an additional payment or refund will be applicable.
